About

Driven front-end developer with experience in customer service, resume writing, and career services. My first exposure to the idea of working in tech was at a resume writing startup. Although in a customer service role, I was curious about what went on behind the scenes on the tech and product teams. I decided to follow this curiosity by completing the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Denver University. Since diving into coding, I’ve come to love every stage in the process of building an app: the initial problem solving and wire-framing of a project, creating the logic with Javascript and jQuery, and transforming the UI with CSS and Bootstrap. Through my experiences, I’ve developed strong skills in communication and cross-team collaboration. Working at a startup trained me to take challenges and problems as they come, and to always strive for improvement. These strengths, combined with my technical skills, make me an advantageous candidate to any creative team.
