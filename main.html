About

Hi! My name is Zoë and I'm a front-end developer. I decided to pursue Web Development out of a curiosity for discovering how technology works and a desire to constantly learn and grow. Since starting the Web Development Bootcamp at Denver University, I’ve come to appreciate every stage in the process of creating an app: the initial problem solving and wire-framing, building functionality with Javascript, transforming the UI with CSS, and solving the inevitable bugs. Through my professional experience in customer service and career services, I’ve learned how to communicate effectively and collaborate in teams to achieve collective goals. The detail-focused mindset I developed as a resume writer and editor lends well to coding. I truly enjoy getting to know my team members and collaborating on projects that make an impact, and am excited to continue doing so in the future.
