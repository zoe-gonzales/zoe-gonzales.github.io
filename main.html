About

Hi! My name is Zoë and I'm a front-end developer with experience in customer service, resume writing, and career services. I decided to pursue Web Development after working at a startup and developing a curiosity for technology. Since enrolling in the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Denver University, I’ve come to love every stage in the process of building an app: the initial problem solving and wire-framing of a project, building the logic with Javascript, and transforming the UI with CSS and Bootstrap. Through my experiences, I’ve developed strong skills in communication and cross-team collaboration. Working at several rapidly growing companies trained me to handle issues quickly, adapt to ongoing changes, and always strive for improvement.
