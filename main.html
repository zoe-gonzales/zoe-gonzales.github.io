About

My name is Zoë and I'm a front-end developer. A curiosity for discovering how technology works and desire to learn and grow every day drove me to this field. Prior to learning web development at the University of Denver, my experience working at quickly growing companies helped me gain strong skills in communication and teamwork. The detail-focused mindset I developed as a writer and editor lends well to coding. I truly enjoy getting to know my team members and collaborating on projects that make an impact, and am excited to continue doing so in the future.
